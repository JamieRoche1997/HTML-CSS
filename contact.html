How much does it cost?

There is no cost to join the club, everything will be 100% free.


How often will there be competitions?

Competitions will take place once a week on Wednesdays at 20:00 - 22:00. You can find our time table via the link here.

Yes! Team nights out is definitely something we're looking further into however, at the moment we're trying to see how many numbers we can get. Once everything is up and running, we will hold a group meeting so we can discuss which nights work best for everyone.
